Writing

the crag (2022)

there's a crag where i live
it starts right outside my door
and i fall in every day

it is deep
and dark and dirty down there
and i can never seem to find my way out
until it is time to sleep again

when i step outside in the morning
the sun pierces my back
and warms my heart
but only briefly

as i step out
into the world
i trip and fall into that crag
that crag that starts right outside my door

there is no sun down here
no light at all
and my mind collects the filth
and the dirt that lines the walls

my only comfort is that everyone else seems to fall into the crag too
whenever they walk outside their own houses in the morning
so we all fumble in the darkness together
collecting filth

how nice, i thought to myself one morning
would it be to have some light in the crag?
to bring down a piece of that blinding sun?
so that people could see the dirt on the walls and their hands and their minds

i stay up late
thinking of a way
to bring down a piece of the sun
into that crag right outside our doors

...

i never could quite figure it out
how to bring a piece of the sun down into the crag
the only solution i found
was to keep myself from falling in

if i look down,
i can see everyone else
down in the crag
shuffling in the darkness

i want to help them
to show them the sun
but i don't have the strength


please give me the strength


   

meaning of me (2022)

i met a man, once
who knew the meaning of the moon
who knew the meaning of the stars and the heavens
the meaning of the earth
the meaning of me

Adam, the man said
take up your shovel, and dig into the bowels of the earth
to that place where men melt and mysteries unveil
Adam, he said
prepare your sacrifice
what do you mean? i cried out to the man
to that man who knew the meaning of the moon
what shall i prepare? how shall i prepare it?
but the man would not answer.
Dig, he said, and was gone



i get lonely, sometimes, in this hole that i dug
it is dark, and i cannot tell if i am digging in the right places
it is not easy work
many times i thought about leaving this hole
this hole that i dug
to go back
back to where i was living before
but i think it is getting hotter
the man visits me, every once and a while
that man who knew the meaning of the stars and the heavens
upon each visit, i ask him again, how i should prepare my special sacrifice
but each time i ask, all that he says
is Dig

some time later
how much later i cannot say
i reached that place where men melt and mysteries are unveiled
and the man came to me, and said
it is time
Time for what? i ask the man
How am I to know what to do?
What is the sacrifice?
You, the man says to me. You.
i melt away,
into bleak oblivion.
into dust.
into nothing.
I am gone.
Gone
but i am also right here.
and i always will be
Always here; never there nor then
always here.

i did go back, eventually.
back to that place where i was before.
but things are different around here now.
i am gone.
melted into the bowels of the earth.
gone.
But I am also right here,
and now I know the meaning of me.


   

the trail (2022)

the trail was right there. but you were too smart for that, too good. you know the way. you think.
you can hop logs, push past bushes. you can climb rocks. you think.

you left the trail a long time ago. it doesn't bother you. too much anymore, anyway.
you got lost a long time ago. it doesnt bother you. you think.
you take another blind step forward, into the forest, into the dark. you can do this. you think.
but you can't. the rocks begin to crack. the trees shake and fall. the lake boils. the bushes burn. you brought yourself here. you found this place. all on your own.
you long for shelter. but there is nowhere to go.
you long for your home, for your bed, for your friends. but there is nowhere to go. nowhere to hide. nowhere to feel safe. nowhere to run. you found this place. all on your own. you hopped the logs, you pushed past the bushes, you climbed the rocks. you left the trail.
this is your home now, next to the cracking rocks and falling trees and boiling lake. this is your home, next to the burning bushes.
this is your hell, and you brought yourself here.
you left the trail.

maybe its not so bad, you say. next to the boiling lake and the burning bushes.
I like it here, you say. a small part of you knows that isn't true, but you keep smiling anyways.
I like it here, you say again, louder. you manage to convince yourself, at least for the moment. but that small part of you remains.
so stubborn. it never leaves you, even though you wish it would. you've done everything you can to make a home of this place. this place by the boiling lake.
you sit and watch time as it passes. you forgot about the trail a long time ago.
but that small part of you twitches and kicks. it reminds you of that time long ago, of that trail you used to walk on.
what would it be like, you dream to yourself, to walk on that trail again? to leave the home that you made by the burning bushes?
you feel compelled to act, to find the trail, to walk it once more.
but you cant. you got lost a long time ago.
you left the trail.


do not be afraid.
gods blessing is given to you also, and you too shall drink from the cup and eat of the bread.
come now, and be glad, for the time has come.
the trail is not lost, for all that seek it will find it once more.
you left the trail, but you found it again.


   
   

mind maze (2019)

mazes can be fun
but not that fun
you build this maze for yourself
but you got lost for a bit too long this time
late for dinner, moms worried

lost in your maze
your very own maze
that you built for yourself
that you built for your mind

laying in bed
just building your maze
if only you knew
what trouble it made

lost again
wait to be found
trapped in your maze
trapped in your mind

get out!
you think
but you cant
i dont think

you built this maze
you built it for you
when it comes from inside
you cant defend against it

----

your maze is your home
your very own new home
that you built for yourself
all by yourself

you spend all this time
thinking alone
trapped in your maze
anxiety maze

maze of melancholy
this wasnt your aim
yours is the blame
yours alone is the blame

you try to forget
you to to escape
but your mind maze
is your new home forever


   

stuck in the stairwell (2019)

up or down
i cannot decide
i am stuck in the stairwell

dad called me up for dinner
but everyone else is still downstairs
playing cards

hunger pulls me higher
but i just cant force myself
to fold

the din from the basement
reminds me of home
but the light from above
bids me beyond

what is more beautiful?
what is more honourable?
to remain and create
or to brave that eastern temple?

body and soul
i am divided


   

how could you have overlooked this (2018)

sad eyes
feign face
disappointment realized
reciprocal rage

laden days
cataracts cured
i approach the source
set myself back

uncertain settlement
chemical sham or
chemical candor
i know i'll choose what's best for me

neural signals
old as we are
transmissions sharpen my intent
sharpen my eyes

chemical potential
for reciprocal regret
reciprocal apathy
reciprocal scorn

samsara letdown
chemical grave town
open your eyes
it's time to go

it's easy to move
but hard to defend
i'm in too deep
i know
